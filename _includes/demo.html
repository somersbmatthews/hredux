How it works

Employer

In the dashboard, press the blue button on the right that says ADD EMPLOYEE.

Add info and press CONFIRM.

Click the compliance tile or click COMPLIANCE in the menu bar at the top.

Scroll down to see all of your paperwork.

Click SUPPORT in the main navigation and select the dropdown to filter FAQ info.

On the dashboard, click UPLOAD PAYROLL FILE.

Upload the corresponding Excel file.

Employee

Scroll to the bottom.

Add info and press CONFIRM.

Use the date selector and press CONFIRM.

Use the number pad to enter your Social Security number.

Read over the contribution information.

Tap on any number to edit it.

Tap any of the three buttons at the bottom for how much time you have to complete onboarding.

To connect with a financial assistant, tap the hamburger menu.

Press ASK US ANYTHING.

Tap CONTRIBUTION in the main navigation at the top.

Scroll and press the button that says, CHANGE CONTRIBUTION.

Tap the hamburger menu.

Tap MY INFORMATION.

To edit, press the pencil button in the top right.

When changes are made, tap the save button.

Tap the left part of the nav bar.

Scroll down to see a summary of your allocation.

To edit, press the button in the bottom right.
